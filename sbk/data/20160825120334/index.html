Hyman Paul Bley, CM was a Canadian pianist known for his contributions to the free jazz movement of the 1960s as well as his innovations and influence on trio playing. Bley was a long-time resident of the United States. Wikipedia
Born: November 10, 1932, Montreal, Canada
Died: January 3, 2016, Stuart, FL
Spouse: Carol Goss (m. 1972–2016), Annette Peacock (m. 1966–1972), Carla Bley (m. 1957–1964)
Children: Vanessa Bley, Angelica Palmer, Solo Peacock
