an·no·ta·tion
ˌanəˈtāSH(ə)n/
noun
noun: annotation; plural noun: annotations
  1. a note of explanation or comment added to a text or diagram.
    "marginal annotations"
    synonyms:note, notation, comment, gloss, footnote; More
    commentary, explanation, interpretation
    "the teacher's copy has annotations in the margins"
    • the action of annotating a text or diagram.
      "annotation of prescribed texts"
Origin
late Middle English: from French, or from Latin annotatio(n-), from the verb annotare (see annotate).
